About Us

We're a neighborhood coffee shop.
The A J Coffee Shop vision is to offer a neighborhood coffee shop serving Fair Trade coffees
derived from plantations around the world, roasted locally and brewed to the highest standards.
. We offer local baked goods and dishes utilizing sustainable ingredients - in some cases grown in our own New England backyard.
The staff and even the art adorning our walls draw upon the talent here in our community.
The A J Coffee shop is local in everyway - originating from the simple desire for the perfect cup of tea or coffee.
We hope that you’ll “Go Green” and pay us a visit.
